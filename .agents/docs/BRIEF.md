# AGENCY BRIEF: The Selling Deck

> This document is the canonical business brief. All copy, positioning, and design decisions MUST align with this brief.

---

## I. Agency Identity

**Positioning**: A specialized digital creative studio bridging **High-Impact Social Media Branding** and **High-Conversion Digital Experiences**.

**Core Narrative**: We don't build websites. We build **Seamless Digital Transitions** — ensuring a brand's website feels like a high-definition extension of its social content, not a step backward.

**Signature Differentiators**:
1. **Musician-Led Flow**: Development rooted in tempo, rhythm, and emotional resonance. We compose digital journeys, not just arrange pixels.
2. **High-Fidelity Motion (60fps)**: Zero-jank animations via Framer Motion. Interfaces that feel alive.
3. **Native Social-to-Site Architecture**: Optimized for in-app browsers (Instagram/TikTok). Zero friction from "Swipe Up" to checkout.

---

## II. The Market Thesis: The "Social-to-Site" Gap

**Context**: In the 2026 Indonesian market, brands are born on Instagram and TikTok. These platforms are "Borrowed Land."

**The Problem**: Most brands suffer from an **Experience Drop-off** — their social media is premium, their website is generic, slow, and "broken."

**Our Solution**: A seamless digital transition where the website captures and converts the brand's social energy.

---

## III. Target Verticals

### Primary: Social-First DTC Brands
- **Who**: Fashion, Skincare, Lifestyle brands with 50k+ followers.
- **They have**: The System (IG/TikTok traffic).
- **They need**: The Engine (a website that handles load and maintains aesthetic).
- **Their pain**: Clunky Link-in-Bio solutions and slow mobile sites that kill conversion.

### Secondary: Experience-Driven Lifestyle ("Aesthetic Brands")
- **Who**: Specialty Coffee Roasteries, Boutique Cafes, Design Studios.
- **Their product**: The atmosphere itself.
- **Their pain**: Static, lifeless websites that fail to communicate sensory experience.

## V. Language
- Primary: **Indonesian (Bahasa Indonesia)**
- Secondary: **English**
- The site must support a seamless **ID/EN language switcher**.
- Copy should feel premium in both languages — avoid direct word-for-word translation. Each version should feel native.
